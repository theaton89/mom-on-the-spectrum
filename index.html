About Me

I'm Taylor, a mom of 2 who was diagnosed with Autism at age 31 in 2020. This news revolutionized my life by helping me better understand myself and the world around me. Since then, I've made it my mission to educate and empower others about the true nature of autism. Autistic people make the world a better place, and it's time to learn how to better support, empower and celebrate autistic individuals.

I post weekly content on my YouTube channel and would love to interact with you in the comments there. You can also find the links to my social media accounts below.
